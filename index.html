United States
B1/B2 Tourist Visa · DS-160

Most fans from our key markets require a B1/B2 visa to enter the US. This is the most in-demand visa for the tournament. FIFA PASS gives ticket holders priority appointment access.

  • ✓DS-160 form completed for you

  • ✓Full interview preparation
🇨🇦
Canada
Visitor Visa or eTA · TRV

Canada requires either an eTA (for eligible passport holders) or a full Temporary Resident Visa (TRV). Requirements depend on your nationality. We assess and handle whichever applies.

  • ✓TRV or eTA assessed & applied
  • ✓Supporting documents prepared
  • ✓Online submission handled
🇲🇽
Mexico
Visitor Permit · FMM

Many fans can enter Mexico visa-free if they hold a valid US, UK, Canadian, or Schengen visa. Others need a full visitor permit. We check your eligibility and handle accordingly.

  • ✓Eligibility check included
  • ✓FMM permit & documentation
  • ✓Guidance for all nationalities
What is FIFA PASS?
Priority Visa Appointments
for Ticket Holders

FIFA PASS — the FIFA Priority Appointment Scheduling System — allows fans who have purchased official World Cup tickets to access accelerated US visa interview appointments. We activate this for all eligible clients.

Faster Interview Slot

Skip the standard queue — FIFA PASS gives you access to priority appointment slots before the tournament begins.

Requires Official Ticket

FIFA PASS is exclusive to fans who purchased tickets directly from FIFA. Our packages include official tickets — so you qualify.

We Handle the Opt-In

We complete the FIFA PASS opt-in form, match it to your DS-160, and schedule your interview. You just show up.

Packages & Booking
How are hospitality packages different from regular tickets?+
A regular ticket gives you a seat. Our packages include everything — visa processing, economy class flights (upgradeable to business), hotel accommodation, ground transport, travel insurance, official match tickets, and a dedicated agent from start to finish.
How does the booking process work?+
Pay 50% upfront with your agent to lock in your package and begin visa processing. The remaining 50% is paid in person when you meet our team and collect your full travel pack — flights, hotel vouchers, and match tickets included.
Will packages cover every match and city?+
We cover all 16 host cities across the full tournament — group stage through to the Final at MetLife Stadium, New York. Due to high demand, we recommend registering your interest early to guarantee your preferred matches and city.
Are the packages refundable?+
Hospitality packages are generally non-refundable given the nature of official event ticketing. All travel packages include Travel Insurance which covers certain cancellation scenarios. Full Terms & Conditions are provided at checkout.
Can I upgrade my flights to Business Class?+
Yes. All packages include Economy Class flights as standard, with a Business Class upgrade available on request. Upgrade pricing is confirmed by your agent after booking based on your departure city and schedule.

Visa & Travel Documents
What is FIFA PASS and do I need it?+
FIFA PASS (FIFA Priority Appointment Scheduling System) is a US government programme that gives official ticket holders access to faster visa interview slots. If you need a US visa and have official tickets, we activate FIFA PASS for you — significantly speeding up the process.
Do I need visas for all 3 countries?+
It depends on your nationality and which matches you're attending. If you're following your team across all 3 host nations, you may need all 3. Many nationalities can enter Mexico visa-free with a valid US or Schengen visa. Your agent assesses this at consultation.
How long does visa processing take?+
Processing times vary by nationality and consulate. With FIFA PASS priority, many clients receive accelerated appointment slots. We start processing immediately after your booking is confirmed with your agent. We strongly recommend booking early to allow maximum processing time.
What documents do I need to provide?+
Your agent provides a full personalised checklist after booking. Typically: valid passport, photographs, financial statements, employment letter, and travel itinerary. We prepare everything — you just supply the source documents.
What if my visa is refused?+
We prepare your application to the highest standard and coach you for the interview to maximise approval chances. In the rare event of refusal, your agent will advise on next steps including reapplication or alternative routes.

Are you an official agent?+
Yes. WC26 Travels is an officially licensed FIFA World Cup 2026™ sales agent, with access to the same exclusive ticket-inclusive inventory as FIFA's authorized global hospitality network. All match tickets are 100% official.
Can I book with a different company?+
You can — but be careful of unofficial resellers. Booking with an officially licensed agent like WC26 Travels guarantees your tickets are authentic, your package is secure, and your visa support comes from specialists with a proven track record.
